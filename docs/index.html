Features

Everything in your flow. Nothing in your way.

Built for engineers who want fast time logging, accurate Jira context, and fewer interruptions throughout the day.

Quick Capture

Press Ctrl+Shift+Space, search a ticket, and start tracking instantly. Always one hotkey away, wherever you are.

Jira Integration

Connect with OAuth, autocomplete sprint tickets, and keep Jira as your source of truth without living in another browser tab.

AI Assistant

Auto-fill descriptions, generate EOD summaries, and choose from six providers for the workflow that already fits your stack.

Smart Nudges

Get gentle reminders for idle time, forgotten timers, and end-of-day wrap-ups exactly when they matter.

Insights & Analytics

See focus time, meeting load, and per-ticket trends so your weekly review is backed by real data instead of guesswork.

Meeting Detection

Detect Teams, Zoom, and Meet calls, log them with context, and avoid losing focus time to manual cleanup later.

How it works

Zero to tracked in one clean desktop flow.

No setup ritual. No context switching. Just install, trigger, and keep moving.

01

Install & connect

Install FlowTrack, sign in, and connect your Jira workspace. Your sprint tickets are ready the moment the app launches.

02

Track with one hotkey

Press Ctrl+Shift+Space from anywhere, pick a ticket, and start the timer without breaking concentration.

03

Get your EOD in one click

Generate a clean end-of-day summary from real session data, then share it in Slack or sync worklogs back to Jira.
